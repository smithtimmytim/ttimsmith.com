Hi there!

I’m Tim Smith, a Latino designer and frontend developer from Saint Paul, MN.

It was ten years ago that I started teaching myself this “web design” thing. In that time, I’ve been privileged to work with companies large and small including Relay FM, Carbon Ads, &Yet, Lullabot, Crush & Lovely, and The Clinton Foundation.

Early this year, I bought a camera and started making videos. In just a month, my YouTube channel had over 100 subscribers. I’ve made videos on technology, gear, and film reviews. I'll keep experimenting with new ideas.

In 2013, I started Brightly Colored, my writing place for design, development, Star Wars, and more. My writing has been published on A List Apart, Net Magazine, and UX Booth among others.
