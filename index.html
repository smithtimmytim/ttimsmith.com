I’m Tim Smith.
A designer, and front-end developer.

I’m a designer and front-end developer. Some of my proudest work is in my portfolio. After work, I spend time with my wife, read comics, watch movies, and then try to write about it. Speaking of which…

I write The Bold Report, a blog about development, technology, comics, basketball, and whatever else I’m interested in.

I designed and built the CMS that powers Tower, Goodstuff FM, and Relay FM, together with Phil Duffy. If you’re interested, you can read more about it here. Bermon Painter invited me to speak at Blend Conf once, and I had a blast. You can watch my talk and other screencasts I’ve made on my YouTube channel.
