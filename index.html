Hi there!

I’m Tim Smith, a Latino designer and frontend developer from Saint Paul, MN.

It was ten years ago that I started teaching myself this “web design” thing. In that time, I’ve been privileged to work with companies large and small including Relay FM, Carbon Ads, &Yet, Lullabot, Crush & Lovely, and The Clinton Foundation.

In 2013, I started The Bold Report, my writing place for design, development, Star Wars, and more. My writing has been published on A List Apart, Net Magazine, and UX Booth among others.

I love speaking and leading workshops. I’d love to speak at your event too. Take a look at other events I’ve spoken at and get in touch.
