I’m Tim Smith.
A designer, front-end developer, and aspiring radio producer.

During the day, I’m a designer and front-end developer at a great company. At night, I spend time with my wife, and work on my podcast. Speaking of which…

I’m the host of For The Record, a show about the personal struggles of successful, creative people. I designed and built the CMS that powers Tower, Goodstuff FM, and Relay FM, together with Phil Duffy. If you’re interested, you can read more about it here.

You can find me procrastiworking on Twitter, occasionally posting shots to Dribbble, or working on my contribution graph on Github. I jam out to my favorite tunes on Rdio, and share pictures of my life on Instagram.
