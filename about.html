I’m Tim Smith, a designer, front-end developer and aspiring radio producer. During the day, I work at a great company. At night, I spend time with my wife, and work on my podcast. Speaking of which…

I’m the host of For The Record, a show about the personal struggles of successful, creative people. I designed and built the CMS that powers Tower, Goodstuff FM, and Relay FM, together with Phil Duffy. It’s specially built for publishing podcasts, and you can read more about it here.

Around the Internet

You can find me procrastiworking on Twitter, occasionally posting shots to Dribbble, or working on my contribution graph on Github. I jam out to my favorite tunes on Rdio, and share pictures of my life on Instagram.

Colophon

This site is published using the amazing Jekyll, hosted by Media Temple, and deployed with the help of Deploy. Fonts are served with Typekit, and posts are written with Byword.

If you’d like to work with me, have me speak at your event, write for your publication, arrange a time to get some coffee, or just say hi, please get in touch. I get a lot of email, but I try to respond quickly.
